
# Database Interview Questions & Answers

## Q: Why not always normalize?
A: Normalization hurts read performance in large systems.

---

## Q: When to denormalize?
A: Read-heavy, reporting, analytics.

---

## Q: Clustered vs Non-Clustered?
A: Clustered defines data order.

---

## Q: SQL vs NoSQL?
A: Depends on consistency vs scale trade-off.
